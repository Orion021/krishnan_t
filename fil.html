About

Who I Am

I'm a Technical Trainer with over three years of experience teaching complex engineering concepts — now making a deliberate move into industry engineering.

I don't just know the tools — I understand why they exist. As a trainer, I think in learning paths and fundamentals. Now I apply that same clarity to engineering problems: building, analyzing, and troubleshooting real systems.

I approach every problem with strong system thinking and a practical mindset. I'm intentionally moving away from purely instructional roles toward environments where I can design, support, secure, and improve real-world systems.

"I am not living my ideal life yet. But I am actively working towards becoming stronger, more capable, and more disciplined — while building a career that values meaningful work over stagnation."
